# Mobile Phase 13 Checklist

1. Launch the Expo app with a valid `EXPO_PUBLIC_API_URL`.
2. Select an active city and confirm the selection persists after reopening the app.
3. Open Home and confirm restaurant cards load from `/api/public/restaurants`.
4. Confirm sponsored restaurants show a visible `Sponsored` badge.
5. Tap a sponsored restaurant and verify it opens the restaurant screen.
6. Open Search, type a restaurant name, and confirm matching results appear.
7. Open a restaurant, browse menu sections, and add at least one item to cart.
8. Open Cart and continue to Checkout.
9. In Checkout, verify payment method selection, promo input, referral input, and loyalty summary all render.
10. Place an order and confirm the Confirmation screen shows order number and payment status.
11. Open Track and confirm timeline, payment status, and driver details load for the new order.
12. Open the Orders tab and confirm real order history loads, including Track and Reorder actions.
